# Coins and Scoring

Coins exist only in Trash: Crown Debt.

Coins are awarded at the end of each round, then spent in the shop.

## Base Coins

| Result | Coins |
| --- | ---: |
| Round winner | 4 |
| Round loser | 1 |

## Bonus Coins

| Bonus | Coins | How To Earn It |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Chain | +1 | Place at least 3 cards in one turn. |
| Discard play | +1 | Place a card taken directly from the discard pile. |
| Face trash | +1 | Trash a jack, queen, or king during the round. |
| Comeback | +2 | Win the round while needing more cards than the opponent. |
| No item used | +1 | Win the round without using an item. |

## Example

If you win a round, place a 3-card chain, and never use an item:

- +4 for winning
- +1 for the chain
- +1 for no item used

Total: **6 coins**

## Why Coins Are Round-Based

Coins are paid after the round instead of every move. This keeps the shop from exploding too fast and makes item choices feel more intentional.
