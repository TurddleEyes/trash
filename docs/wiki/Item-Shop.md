# Item Shop

The item shop appears between rounds in Trash: Crown Debt.

You can buy items, replace old items, claim Coin Purse, use shop-only items, or skip to the next round.

## Item Rules

- You can hold 2 items.
- If you buy an item while full, you choose an item to replace.
- If you win a round while holding 2 items, you must discard 1 item before shopping.
- Some items are used during your turn.
- Some items are used only in the shop.
- Some items are passive.

## Item List

| Item | Tier | Cost | When Used | Effect |

| Burn Pile | Common | 5 | Before drawing | Removes the top discard and flips a fresh deck card onto the discard pile. |

| Royal Pardon | Uncommon | 7 | Current card | Discard an unplayable current card and draw again. |
| Grave Grab | Uncommon | 8 | Current card | Trash your unplayable current card and take the previous discard. |

| Sabotage | Rare | 12 | Your turn | Flip one random opponent placed card face-down. |
| Chaos Cut | Rare | 11 | Before drawing | Shuffle every face-down card on both boards. |

| Wild Seal | Rare | 12 | Current card | Place one face card into any empty slot. |
| Debt of the Crown | Legendary | 28 | Shop | Add 1 required card to the opponent's board, unless Crown Shield blocks it. |

## Shop Odds

Each shop shows 3 offers.

| Offer Slot | Possible Items |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Common |
| 2 | Common |
| 3 | Uncommon, rare, or legendary |

The third offer has these rules:

- Starting on round 3, Debt of the Crown has an 8 percent chance to appear.
- If Debt does not appear, the third offer has a 35 percent chance to be rare.
- Otherwise, the third offer is uncommon.

## Item Notes

### Coin Purse

Coin Purse is free and gives +3 coins, but it locks the shop for the rest of that between-round phase.

### Lucky Match

Lucky Match only works if one of your hidden cards is already in the right slot. It can win the round if it flips your final needed slot.

### Royal Pardon

Royal Pardon only works when your current card cannot be placed.

### Grave Grab

Grave Grab only works when your current card cannot be placed and the discard pile has a card to take.

### Wild Seal

Wild Seal turns a face card into a playable wild-style placement. After placing it, the card underneath becomes your new current card.

### Debt of the Crown

Debt of the Crown cannot push a player above 10 required cards. If the target is already at 10, it has no effect.
