# Trash: Crown Debt

Trash: Crown Debt is the expanded version of Trash Cards.

It uses the same basic card rules as Classic Trash, but adds coins, shop items, and stronger comeback mechanics.

## Round Flow

1. Play a normal round of Trash.
2. The round winner needs one fewer card next round.
3. Coins are awarded to both players.
4. If the winner holds 2 items, they must discard 1 item.
5. The shop opens.
6. Buy items, use shop items, or skip.
7. Start the next round.

## Inventory Limit

You can hold up to 2 items.

If you try to buy a third item, you choose one of your current items to replace.

## Winner Tax

If you win a round while holding 2 items, you must throw away 1 item before the shop opens.

This keeps the winner from snowballing too hard.

## Shop Offers

Each shop gives 3 offers.

- First offer: common item.
- Second offer: common item.
- Third offer: usually uncommon or rare.
- Starting on round 3, there is a small chance for Debt of the Crown to appear.

## Legendary Item

**Debt of the Crown** can appear after round 3.

It costs 28 coins and adds 1 required card to the opponent's board, up to a maximum of 10 cards.

Example:

If Bot only needs 7 cards next round, Debt of the Crown can push Bot back up to 8.

**Crown Shield** blocks Debt of the Crown automatically.
